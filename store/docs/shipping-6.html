Overview

Here you can batch update your zone tax rates. Adjustments can be applied to all tax rates or only tax rates in selected zones. This is useful if any governments adjust tax levies in yours or other Countries

Tax Rate Adjustment

Specify rate adjustment. This can be a set amount or a percentage for increases or decreases OR a fixed amount. Examples:

10% (For Increase or Decrease. Adds 10% to existing rate.)
17% (Fixed - New fixed rate)

Adjustment Type

Please choose:

Increase or Decrease - Takes existing tax rate and increases or decreases tax rate by amount specified.

Fixed - A new fixed tax rate.

Apply to Tax Rates in Selected Zones

Specify which zones to apply this adjustment to. Use the checkboxes to make your selections.
