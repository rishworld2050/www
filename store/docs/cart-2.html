Products

Displays product and details. Basic product view is as follows:

Recommend or Share this Product Information

If AddThis module is enabled in settings, provides many bookmarking options for the product page.

Product Enquiry

Option for visitors to contact store owner about product. Loads contact page with product link.

Product MP3



May not always appear. When present provides a pop up div of mp3 previews. Useful when selling music items. MP3 previews controlled by MP3 Player.

Product Video



May not always appear. When present displays product video in pop up div. Video settings controlled by FlowPlayer.

Buy Options

A number of buy options (attributes) may also appear beneath the product. These are displayed in drop down menus. If applicable, additional cost may also be shown next to each attribute:

Personalisation Options

If enabled for a product, personalisation options may also appear on a product page. Some of these may be required fields and will be shown as such. If applicable, additional cost may also be shown next to each attribute



In some cases these options may be required. If options have character limit, a count will appear when you start typing:

Add to Cart

This adds the product to the cart. If a personalisation option is required and hasn`t been completed, an error will occur.



If a product has been added to the cart ok, it will appear in the left hand shopping basket.



More info on the shopping basket here.

Image Preview and Zoom

Hovering a cursor over the displayed image initialising the Cloud Zoom option as shown below:



Move the cursor to view the image. The 'Click to Zoom' link displays the full size image in a pop up div. Clicking any small thumbnails, changes the main display image.
