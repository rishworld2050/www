Menu Basket

Maian Cart has 2 shopping basket features. Firstly, a basket in the left hand menu so visitors can see at a glance whats in their shopping cart. This is useful as it means they don`t have to keep viewing the checkout page. The second is the checkout page itself which is more complex. This page details the menu basket.

Adding to Basket

When an 'Add to Cart' link has been clicked, the item appears in the menu basket as follows:



The menu basket is controlled via Ajax and the jQuery functions.

Removing Items

Clicking the 'X' next to a product name removes it from the basket.

Cart Reload

If all items have been removed from the menu basket, it resets to its original state.

Items in Cart

Besides the menu basket, the total items in the cart is also auto refreshed at the top of the page.

Checkout & Pay

Clicking this link takes a visitor to the checkout page.
