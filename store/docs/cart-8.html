Shipping Selectors v Standard

Note that the Maian Cart checkout page has 2 similar, but slightly different displays depending on whether you have shipping options in place. If you have shipping options, visitors are required to choose shipping options before they can checkout, where as no shipping options loads all the other cart options by default. Try it with and without zones to see the difference.

Checkout

There are many aspects to the Maian Cart checkout routine, each of which are detailed below. When you load the checkout page you`ll see items in your cart as follows:

Understanding Checkout Routine

The only time a visitor will see items in their basket is when they are in the store area. As soon as they are transferred to a payment gateway (ie: Paypal), they will NOT see their items listed in a basket format. This is because Maian Cart does not utilise gateway cart options, but instead processes the payment as a single entity. After payment a unique code is transmitted back to the store to update the purchases and send e-mails.

Once you test the system you`ll understand it better.

Clear Cart

The 'Clear Cart' link clears all items in cart.

Remove Item

Clicking the 'Remove' link beneath a product removes that item from the basket. Clicking the quantity adjustment icon will also trigger a delete prompt if it reaches 0. Click 'OK' at the prompt to delete or 'Cancel' to ignore:

Changing Quantities

Use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease quantity. Product price will auto update as will the quantity next to the product name. If you exceed a stock level an error will appear.

Product Attributes

If you added any buy options to your cart, these are shown beneath the product name. Price is inclusive of additional costing:

Personalised Option

If you have added a personalised item to your cart, it will appear with the personalised item info beneath the product.



You can edit/update your personalised items directly on the checkout page by clicking the 'Edit Personalisation' link. This launches a pop up with the edit options:



Clicking to close this window refreshes the personalised options on the checkout page. There are NO options to delete just the personalisation. If you wish to completely remove this, delete product from cart.

Shipping

The shipping selectors only apply if shipping zones are set. These will not appear if you have no zones. Choose your country and area/region to load available shipping options if applicable:



Radio buttons are shown next to each option. Choose the option you prefer. To reload shipping options, click 'Change Selection'.

CHANGING QUANTITIES OR PERSONALISED DATA WILL AUTO RE-LOAD SHIPPING OPTIONS & OTHER CHECKOUT OPTIONS IF APPLICABLE

Clicking a radio button loads the rest of the checkout page.

Discount Coupons

Checkout & Pay

Once you are happy with your order, choose a payment option and click 'Checkout & Pay'.



If enabled, some payment options may display another screen asking for name/address details. For example, bank transfers or phone/cheque payments. This screen displays:



Complete the info before completing order.

Paying

Depending on which payment method you chose, you should follow the on screen instructions to complete payment.

Cancelling Payments

Options are provided by each gateway to cancel a payment. If this is clicked, visitor is returned to store and no payment is sent or processed.
