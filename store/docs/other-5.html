Key Front End Features

Straight to payment method shopping cart system, no account signups.
Supports Paypal, 2Checkout, AlertPay, Moneybookers & Google Checkout.
None payment processing methods also supported.
XHTML strict 99.9% tableless layout. Fully web compliant.
Ajax cart system consolidated onto single checkout page.
Disable checkout system. Ideal if you want to showcase your products, but not sell online.
Templated system using Savant2/3 template engines.
Categories/sub categories.
Category icons.
Products can be in multiple categories.
Unlimited products and product attributes/attribute groups.
MP3 previews if selling CDs.
Supports tangible and downloadable products.
Latest news ticker.
Consolidates local downloads into single zip file if enabled.
Downloads can be stored outside of web root for protection from download managers.
Unlimited product images.
Product tags.
Product brands.
Product video.
Related products.
Price points.
Block products from being purchased from certain countries.
MP3 previews for music items.
Product personalisation with max character restrictions and input types.
Newsletter signup option with subscribe/unsubscribe options.
Bookmark and share with AddThis.
Newsletter opt-in feature on checkout.
Discount coupon system with fixed, percentage, free shipping ONLY & free tax ONLY options.
Latest products and special offers.
Fully searchable.
Currency converter.
Contact page & unlimited additional pages.
Most popular products list and also recently viewed items.
Customers who bought this bought that feature.
Low stock level e-mail notification.
